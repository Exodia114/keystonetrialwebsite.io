HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

Keystone - Multi Purpose Cooperatve Society Limited, bearing Registration Number 1902,is registered with the Ministry of Cooperatives,since 28th January 2011. The Society is situated at Royal Road, Petite Riviere. It operates on an island wide basis. Members are encouraged to enroll other members of their family as shareholders.
