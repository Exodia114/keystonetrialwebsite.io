WHO WE ARE?

Keystone Multi-Purpose Cooperative Society Ltd known as Keystone Co-operative Credit union Limited was registered in January 2011 having registration No 1902. We started with six members and now we have ninety two members. Keystone CCU merged with Petite-Riviere store and become Keystone Multi Purpose co-operative Society Ltd on 12th September 2014. The former was in liquidation and the liquidator was PCO Beekarry. We worked very hard together to save the only “Patrimoine cooperative of Petite Riviere which is dated 1948 that is 70 years back. Keystone Multi-Purpose Cooperative Society Ltd is a member-owned financial one, democratically controlled by its members, and operated for the purpose of promoting thrift, providing credit at competitive rates. We promote best practices and good governance in the cooperative. Besides all board members are degree holders. We involve in social, economic and environmental development whilst adopting innovation, technology and sustainable practices. We encourage members to continually improve towards excellence so that we can give more visibility to cooperative movement.
